Good to know

  • Free to use

    There's no charge to use Seller Hub. You only pay for any subscriptions you may have, such as Selling Manager Pro or Stores, to access that functionality from Seller Hub.

  • Powerful reporting

    Get valuable insights into your business performance. In just a few clicks, find out how many buyers see and visit your listings, what your daily selling costs are, and how often links to your listings are put in front of buyers.

  • The tools you love and more

    We consolidated the features of your current selling tool—My eBay, Selling Manager, or Selling Manager Pro—and added new ways to look at your sales, selling costs, and buyer traffic. Stores subscribers can also find powerful marketing and promotion tools.

  • US only for now

    Seller Hub is currently only available to US sellers who list on eBay.com. We're working hard to make Seller Hub available on our international sites, too.

  • All your current listings and orders

    If you're already selling on eBay, we bring over all your listing activity and orders from your current selling tool, so you can just jump right in. You won't have to re-enter anything.

  • Ready to start?

    After you opt in, just click Sell on any eBay page to get to Seller Hub.

  • Easy to switch

    If you decide you want to go back to your old selling tool—My eBay, Selling Manager, or Selling Manager Pro—it's easy to switch back. Don't worry, you can opt back in to Seller Hub at any time.
